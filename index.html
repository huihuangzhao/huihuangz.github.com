Biography

I received my Ph.D. degree in 2010 from XiDian University(advised by Prof. De-Jian Zhou). I am currently an Associate Professor (Graduate Supervisor) and the leader of Institute of Intelligent Computing and Graphic & Image Processing, Hengyang Normal University, Hengyang, Hunan, China. My current research interests include Computer Vision, Compressive Sensing and Machine Learning.
